The Philosophy & Saarthi

Meditation is Already Within You

The journey of Umesh Misra & iChaitanya.

iChaitanya is built on the realization that meditation is not something to achieve, force, or artificially create.

Moments of silence, stillness, thoughtlessness, peace, deep love, awareness, and complete presence are already glimpses of meditation.

"Most people simply do not recognize these quiet spaces as meditation itself. They are waiting for some complex lightning event. But meditation is much simpler—it is your natural resting state."

iChaitanya is a Dhyaan (Meditation) journey that helps people consciously return to that calmness, silence the mind, go beyond it, and discover the peace, strength, awareness, and creation already within.

About Umesh Misra (Saarthi)

"The role of a Saarthi is not to preach"

iChaitanya was born from years of direct, silent meditative experience. Umesh Misra guides meditation not with rigid dogmas or dry textbook preaching, but by matching your immediate emotional states.

A Saarthi (charioteer) does not dictate your destiny—he simply guides your carriage safely back toward the stillness you have already experienced inside.
